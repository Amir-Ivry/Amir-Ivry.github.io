I’m a postdoctoral researcher tackling what I call the broken compass problem in speech systems: when evaluation is ambiguous and slows innovation instead of driving it. My work seeks to rebuild that compass by grounding evaluation in human perception-enabling more reliable speech, music, and audio systems across robotics, assistive technologies, and human-machine interactions.

I've been an industry Research Scientist since 2015, building and leading projects addressing challenges in speech, language, vision, and time-series modalities. Most recently, I served as Principal Researcher and Head of the Data Domain in Microsoft Teams (2021–2025). In parallel, I pursued a direct-track PhD at the Technion (2018–2023), advancing efficient personalization in speech systems.
