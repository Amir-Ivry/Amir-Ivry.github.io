I’m a postdoctoral researcher tackling what I call the broken compass problem in speech systems: when evaluation is ambiguous, misguides progress, and slows innovation instead of accelerating it. My work seeks to rebuild that compass - making speech, music, and audio systems more reliable by anchoring evaluation in human perception.

I've been an industry research scientist since 2015, building and leading projects addressing speech, language, vision, and time-series challenge. Most recently, I served as Principal Researcher and Head of the Data Domain in Microsoft Teams (2021–2025). In parallel, I pursued a direct-track PhD at the Technion (2018–2023), exploring efficient personalization in speech systems.
